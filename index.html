Frequently Asked Questions

How can I find a yoga class near me?

Simply search by location, class type, or instructor on our homepage. You can browse through a variety of yoga classes in your area and choose the one that best fits your schedule and needs.

How can I manage my yoga studio as a studio owner?

As a studio owner, you can sign up and create a profile for your studio. From there, you’ll be able to manage class schedules, update class descriptions, promote your events, and interact with students. You’ll also have access to tools that make managing your studio more efficient.

Is there a fee for studio owners ?

We offer various subscription plans, including a free option with basic features. Premium plans unlock additional features such as advanced scheduling tools, marketing options, and detailed analytics to help you grow your studio.

How do I book a yoga class?

Once you’ve found a class that interests you, click on the class details and select "Book Now." You can then complete the booking process by providing your details and payment information if required.

Can I offer online classes through ?

Yes, our platform supports online classes. You can list virtual classes, provide streaming links, and allow students to book and attend classes from anywhere.

How do I promote my yoga studio on the platform?

You can promote your studio by creating a compelling profile, offering special promotions, and showcasing your classes and events. Additionally, our premium plan offers marketing tools to boost your studio’s visibility.
